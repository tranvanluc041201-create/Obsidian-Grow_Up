## 4) 
### Original

“Which is the most suitable ai for obsidian?The ai request:1st.match the task,2ed.capacity 3rd.cost-effective”

### Issues → why → corrected version

1. **Capitalization (大写)& spacing(间隔)**
    

- Issue: “ai”, “obsidian”, missing spaces after punctuation(标点)
    
- Why: Proper nouns and sentence boundaries() improve readability
    
- Fix: **“Which AI is most suitable for Obsidian?”**
    

2. **Word choice (“request”)**
    

- Issue: “The ai request” sounds unnatural
    
- Why: You’re describing criteria(要求/标准)/priorities
    
- Fix: **“My priorities are:”**
    

3. **Ordinal words**
    

- Issue: “2ed” should be “2nd”
    
- Why: Standard English ordinals: 1st/2nd/3rd
    
- Fix: **“1st…, 2nd…, 3rd…”**
    

### Polished version

“Which AI is most suitable for Obsidian? My priorities are: (1) task fit, (2) capability, and (3) ***cost-effectiveness*.**”

Your text

“Here are three choices：1st glm4.7，2nd kimi 2.5，3rd MiniMax 2.1 Pleases based on the priorities（don´t take the cost effectiveness into account） above and recommend which Ai is most suitable for OpenClaw”

### Fixes

1. **“Pleases” → “Please”**
    
    
- Better: **“Please recommend…”**
    

1. **Punctuation(标点) & spacing**
    

- Why: Full-width punctuation and missing spaces reduce readability in English.
    
- Better: Use normal commas/colons(冒号) and add spaces after punctuation.
    

3. **Capitalization**
    

- Why: “AI” is typically capitalized; model names should be consistent.
    
- Better: **“GLM-4.7, Kimi K2.5, MiniMax M2.1”**
    

### Polished version

**“Here are three options: (1) GLM-4.7, (2) Kimi K2.5, and (3) MiniMax M2.1. *Based on my priorities above* (ignore cost-effectiveness), which AI is most suitable for OpenClaw?”**
